About Hindi Ki Neev
What is Hindi Ki Neev?
Hindi Ki Neev (HKN) is a community Hindi language school serving families in the Bay Area. Our mission is to help children of Indian origin develop a strong foundation in Hindi — reading, writing, and speaking — in a nurturing, structured environment.
Who teaches at HKN?
Our teachers are experienced, passionate educators who are native or fluent Hindi speakers. Each class is led by a dedicated teacher who guides students through our structured curriculum.
How long has HKN been operating?
Hindi Ki Neev has been serving the Bay Area community for over a decade, helping hundreds of children build a lifelong connection to the Hindi language and Indian culture.

Enrollment & Fees
How do I enroll my child?
Enrollment is managed through our online Student Portal. New families register their interest, and returning families re-enroll each year. Once a spot is confirmed, you will receive an invitation to complete enrollment and submit payment.
What are the fees?
The annual enrollment fee covers all three sessions (Fall, Winter, and Spring). Please check the portal or contact us at info@hindikineev.org for the current fee amount.
How do I pay?
We accept payment via Zelle. Instructions and the payment details are shown in the Student Portal when you complete enrollment.
What is the refund policy?
Refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis. Please contact us as early as possible if your circumstances change. Generally, refunds are not available after classes have begun.

Classes & Schedule
When and where are classes held?
Classes are held on Saturday mornings at our regular venue in the Bay Area. Specific dates, times, and location details are communicated to enrolled families before each session begins.
How long is each class?
Each class session is approximately one hour long.
How many classes are in each session?
Each session (Fall, Winter, Spring) typically includes 10–11 class dates. The exact dates are listed in the Student Portal under your child's session information.
What if my child misses a class?
Please inform your child's teacher in advance if they will be absent. Attendance is tracked and visible to you in the Student Portal. Consistent attendance is important for progress — we encourage families to prioritize class attendance.
What if a class is cancelled?
In case of a cancellation (e.g. holiday, unforeseen circumstances), families will be notified by email. The school makes every effort to minimize cancellations.

Curriculum & Levels
What curriculum does HKN use?
HKN uses a structured, graded curriculum with dedicated textbooks and exercise books for each level. The program progresses from foundational literacy (letters, sounds) through reading, writing, grammar, and conversational Hindi.
What are the class levels?
Our program has nine levels across three bands:
  • Beginner (Beg-1, Beg-2, Beg-3) — letter recognition, phonics, basic vocabulary
  • Intermediate (Int-1, Int-2, Int-3, Int-4) — reading, writing, grammar
  • Advanced (Adv-1, Adv-2) — fluency, composition, literature
Each level typically takes one full school year (three sessions) to complete. Students are assessed and placed at the appropriate level when they join.
What books does my child need?
Books are provided by the school and distributed at orientation. Each level uses a specific Textbook and Exercise Book. Students who continue from one level to the next within the same book may keep their book — no new book is needed until they advance to the next book level.
How is my child assessed?
Teachers assess students on three skills — Reading, Writing, and Speaking — at the end of each session. Scores are visible in the Student Portal.

Homework & Progress
Is homework assigned?
Yes — teachers assign regular homework from the exercise book. Completing homework consistently is one of the most important factors in a student's progress in Hindi.
How can I support my child's learning at home?
  • Encourage daily practice — even 10–15 minutes makes a big difference
  • Help your child complete their exercise book homework on time
  • Speak Hindi at home when possible
  • Read Hindi books or watch age-appropriate Hindi content together
  • Celebrate small milestones — learning a new script or reading their first sentence
How do I know how my child is progressing?
Progress is tracked in three ways:
  • Attendance — visible in the Student Portal for each session
  • Scores — Reading, Writing, and Speaking scores updated by teachers
  • Teacher feedback — reach out to your child's teacher directly with questions

Parent Responsibilities
What is expected of parents?
  • Ensure your child attends classes regularly and on time
  • Inform the teacher in advance of any planned absences
  • Ensure homework is completed before each class
  • Keep contact information up to date in the Student Portal
  • Pay enrollment fees on time
  • Communicate proactively with teachers about any concerns
How do I contact my child's teacher?
Teacher contact details are shared with enrolled families. You can also reach us at info@hindikineev.org and we will connect you with the right person.
What is the code of conduct?
We expect all students to be respectful to teachers and fellow students, come prepared with their books and completed homework, and maintain a positive attitude toward learning. Students who are disruptive may be asked to sit out of class activities.

Checking Your Child's Progress Online
How do I access the Student Portal?
Visit the HKN Student Portal and enter your parent email address, your child's first name, and date of birth. If your child is enrolled, you will see their current session information, attendance record, and assessment scores.
🔗 Access the portal at: www.hindikineev.org — look for the Student Portal link on the website.
What information is available in the portal?
  • Your child's current enrollment status
  • Current session — teacher, class time, class level, and book
  • Attendance record for the current session
  • Reading, Writing, and Speaking scores
  • Enrollment confirmation and payment status
My child's information is not showing up — what do I do?
Please make sure you are entering the same email address and date of birth that were provided during enrollment. If you continue to have trouble, contact us at info@hindikineev.org.
